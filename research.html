My research studies how large language models and LLM agents can retrieve, extract, and use information at inference time, especially for long-context tasks where relevant evidence is distributed across documents and has complex dependencies.

I have worked on coding agents as long-context processors, single-pass document scanning for question answering with state space models, information retrieval benchmarks with complex objectives, and biomedical benchmarks for evidence extraction and risk-of-bias assessment.
